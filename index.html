What is the best mobile game?

Call of Duty Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter for mobile devices, offering both classic multiplayer modes (like Team Deathmatch and Domination) and a battle royale mode with 100 players. It features iconic maps, customizable weapons, and a range of operator skills and perks. The game offers high-quality graphics, smooth controls, and frequent updates with new content. Players can engage in ranked matches, join clans, and unlock rewards through a Battle Pass. With its fast-paced action and extensive game modes, Call of Duty: Mobile delivers a console-like FPS experience on mobile devices.

Who Invented Call of duty Mobile?

Call of Duty: Mobile was developed by TiMi Studios, a subsidiary of Tencent Games, in collaboration with Activision. It was officially released on October 1, 2019. The game was designed to bring the Call of Duty franchise's iconic first-person shooter action to mobile devices, combining traditional multiplayer modes with a battle royale experience. While TiMi Studios handled the development, Activision, the publisher of the Call of Duty series, provided the game's intellectual property and oversight.
